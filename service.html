About SociallyGood
SociallyGood is a digital Marketplace to craft social change. It is a technology-enabled online engagement platform bringing together the main stakeholders including Non-Profits, Corporates and Individuals as Volunteers and Donors.

With an aim to increase the global reach, global engagement and efficiency for all the players, SociallyGood has adopted best of technologies including Artificial Intelligence and integrated more than 10-point solutions into one robust platform to make fundraising, volunteering, event and beneficiary management simple.

SociallyGood was started with the aim to empower urban and rural people across the world to make a measurable social impact for the greater good. SociallyGood has already hundreds of Non-Profits on its marketplace from India and United States, and targeting to have 100k Non-Profits by 2022 and over 1.0 million Non-Profits & Corporates by 2024.

We are hopeful and excited about making the world a better place with SociallyGood.
